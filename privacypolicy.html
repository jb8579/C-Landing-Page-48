Privacy Policy

Introduction

At Sleeper Games, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our website and services, including our fantasy football, fantasy basketball, and Bracket Mania offerings.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information from you in several ways:

Information You Provide Directly:

  • When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, username, and password.

  • When you participate in fantasy leagues or Bracket Mania contests, we collect your team selections and other related information.

  • When you contact our customer support, we collect your name, email address, and any other information you provide.

  • Payment information, when purchasing any paid services.

Information We Collect Automatically:

  • We collect information about your device and browser, including your IP address, operating system, and browser type.

  • We use cookies and similar technologies to collect information about your activity on our website, such as the pages you visit and the links you click.

  • Information about your gameplay, and interactions within the application.

Information from Third Parties:

  • We may receive information from third-party social media platforms if you choose to connect your account with those platforms.

  • We may receive analytical information from third-party analytics providers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and improve our services, including fantasy football, fantasy basketball, and Bracket Mania.

  • To personalize your experience on our website.

  • To communicate with you about our services, including updates, promotions, and customer support.

  • To analyze how our website is used and to improve its functionality.

  • To prevent fraud and ensure the security of our website.

  • To comply with legal obligations.

3. Information Sharing

We may share your information with the following third parties:

  • Service providers who assist us in providing our services, such as hosting providers, payment processors, and analytics providers.

  • Third-party social media platforms if you choose to connect your account with those platforms.

  • Law enforcement or other government agencies if required by law.

  • In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of our business.

  • With other users, in the context of gameplay, such as displaying usernames, and league information.

4. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to collect information about your activity on our website. You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may affect your ability to use certain features of our website.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration. However, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • You can access and update your account information at any time.

  • You can request to delete your account.

  • You can opt out of receiving marketing communications from us.

  • You can request a copy of the data we hold about you.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

8. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on our website.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: info@sleeperdfs.com
