Cookie Policy

Introduction

At Sleeper Games, we value your privacy and are committed to safeguarding your personal information. This Cookie Policy explains how we use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience when you visit our website.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help us collect information about your browsing activity, such as which pages you view, links you click, and other interactions. Cookies also enable us to personalize content and ads based on your interests.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

We use the following types of cookies on our website:

  • Necessary Cookies: Essential for the basic operation of our website and cannot be disabled.

  • Preference Cookies: Help remember your preferences, such as language settings.

  • Analytics Cookies: Used to gather data on how visitors interact with our site to help improve functionality.

  • Advertising Cookies: Allow us to display personalized advertisements based on your interests.

3. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for various purposes, including:

  • Enhancing your experience by personalizing content and providing relevant suggestions.

  • Analyzing website traffic to improve our services and performance.

  • Delivering targeted advertisements based on your browsing history.

  • Enabling features like remembering your login or preferences for a smoother experience.

4. Managing Cookies

You have control over cookies and can manage them through your browser settings. You can block or delete cookies, but please note that disabling them may limit certain features on our website.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access. However, no method of internet transmission or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Changes to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy periodically. If we make significant changes, we will notify you by posting the updated policy on this page.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding our Cookie Policy, please reach out to us at: info@sleeperdfs.com
